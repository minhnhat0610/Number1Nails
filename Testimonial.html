Testimonials

What Our Clients Talk About Us

Justin D.

Rating: 4/5

I'm very picky about what nail salons I go to especially in small towns. Im a bit of a nail snob since I'm originally from Las Vegas and used to the salons there. I walked in and was pleasantly surprised. They don't accept appointments, but there is barely a wait and the sign in sheet is right up front. I got a shellac mani w a paraffin dip for $35. I sat down and was at ease when I saw the cuticle cutter, pusher, and other tools soaking in the blue cleaning solution. There's a nice selection of gel colors. Holly did my nails. She was really good, kept checking in with me to make sure I liked the shape and length. She was careful with the cuticle cutter and gentle with the cuticle pusher. I wish the hand massage was a little longer and all the nail polishes were in one place. But this is a great place glad I found it. But even after my service, another friend also recommended it to me as well. I'll definitely be back! - Yelp reviewer
